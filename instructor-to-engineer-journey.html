From Instructor to Full-Stack Engineer: My Journey

Teaching 100+ students at Kalpana Chawla Institute taught me more about coding than I expected.

Explaining concepts clearly forced me to understand them deeply. When I joined Onmouseclick as a Software Engineer, I was ready because I had already solved hundreds of real student problems.

Key Lessons from Teaching

  • Clear communication is as important as clean code
  • Debugging other people's code prepares you for production issues
  • Project-based learning is the fastest way to grow
